## Purpose of Application
This application is a specialized tool designed for Columbia Transport LLC drivers and customers to facilitate the digital management of freight paperwork, including:
- PDF annotation and signing.
- Document scanning and image attachment.
- Load packet generation and sharing.

## Privacy & Data Security
- **Local-First Storage**: This application is built with a "Privacy First" architecture. All data, including signatures, uploaded documents, and personal information, is stored **locally on your device** in the browser's persistent storage (Vault).
- **No Cloud Uploads**: Columbia Transport LLC does not automatically upload your paperwork to a central server. Data is only transmitted when you explicitly use the "Share," "Email," or "SMS" functions to send a completed packet.
- **Data Deletion**: Clearing your browser's cache or local storage will permanently delete your saved vault items. Users are responsible for downloading or sharing their final documentation before clearing their browser data.
